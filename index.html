Conclusion

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple but effective platform to test AI strategies. Minimax ensures optimal moves, Alpha-Beta improves efficiency, and Q-Learning shows adaptability. The comparison shows that AI consistently outperforms humans by thinking faster, more accurately, and learning from experiences. This highlights the strength of AI not just in games, but also in real-world strategic decision-making.
